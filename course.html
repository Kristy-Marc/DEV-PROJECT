Digital Literacy Training

What is Digital Literacy Training?

Digital literacy training involves teaching people how to use computers, the internet, and other digital tools to perform everyday tasks. This includes everything from basic computer skills to more advanced topics like cybersecurity and digital communication.

Benefits of Digital Literacy Training

  • Improved Employability: Many jobs require basic digital skills, so being digitally literate can enhance job prospects.
  • Enhanced Daily Life: Digital skills make it easier to perform everyday tasks like online shopping, banking, and accessing information.
  • >Increased Confidence: Knowing how to use digital tools can boost confidence and independence.
  • Better Communication: Digital literacy enables better communication with friends, family, and colleagues through various online platforms .
